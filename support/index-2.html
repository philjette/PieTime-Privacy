Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not receiving the daily notification?

First, check that notifications are enabled for Pie Time on your device. Go to Settings → Notifications → Pie Time and make sure notifications are turned on. Also make sure Do Not Focus modes aren't blocking them.

The notification says it's Pie Time but the recipe won't load

This is usually a temporary network issue. Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, check that your device has an active internet connection.

I keep seeing the same recipe

This is by design — Pie Time generates one new recipe per day and caches it so it loads quickly. A fresh recipe will appear the next day.

When exactly does the new recipe appear?

A new recipe is generated each calendar day. If you open the app before 3:14 PM you'll see the day's recipe early — the notification at 3:14 PM is just a friendly reminder that it's there.

The app crashed or something looks broken

Try deleting and reinstalling the app. If the issue continues, please reach out to us directly and describe what happened — we'd love to fix it.
